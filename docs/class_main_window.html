Detailed Description

Mainwindow class, that contains implementation of functions of two tabs: homepage and statisitcs. Connects 2 additional tabs: portfolio and figi database. Homepage tab gives an opportunity to filter list of favourite shares by their trading status. Note that list of favourite shares is read-only. Moreover, you can view candles of these shares and plot SMA and EMA for them. Statistics tab gives information about 5 best and worst companies by the volume of their shares in the chosen period: 1 day, 1 week, 1 month.
